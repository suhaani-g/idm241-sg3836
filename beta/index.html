Rules

Product Card:

Hover On

  • The image slightly scales up
  • The image brightness increases
  • The Image fades from bottle to face over .5 seconds

Hover Off

  • The image slightly scales down
  • The image brightness deceases
  • The image fades from face to bottle over .5 seconds

Add to Bag Button:

Hover On

  • The button slightly moves up
  • The button's color fades to light pink
  • The button's stroke turns to dashed

Hover Off

  • The button slightly moves down
  • The button's color fades to white
  • The button's stroke turns to solid
